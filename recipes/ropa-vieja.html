How to make delicious Cuban Ropa Vieja at home

Cuban Ropa Vieja

Making Cuban Ropa Vieja at home is easy and delicious! Simply follow these easy steps and you will be enjoying your Cuban Ropa Vieja in no time!

Ingredients:

  • 1tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2lbs flank steak (or beef cut of choice)
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 (8oz)can tomato sauce
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1 bundle cilantro
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 (6oz)can tomato paste
  • 1tsp cumin
  • 1tbs olive oil
  • 1tbs white vinegar

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Brown the flank steak on each side, about 4 mins per side.
  2. Transfer beef to slow cooker.
  3. Pour in the beef broth and tomato sauce, then add the onion, bell pepper, garlic, tomato paste, cumin, cilantro, olive oil, and vinegar.
  4. Stir until well blended.
  5. Cover and cook for 4hrs on high or up to 10hrs on low.
  6. Shred meat and serve with rice or tortillas.
  7. ENJOY!
