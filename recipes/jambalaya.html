How to make delicious Jambalaya at home

Zatarain's Jambalaya

Making Jambalaya at home is simple and delicious! Simply follow these easy steps and you will be enjoying your tasty jambalaya in no time!

Ingredients:

  • One 12oz box Zatarain's Jambalaya rice mix
  • One 12oz pack of Aidell's Andouille sausage
  • 4oz fresh shrimp, peeled and tailed
  • One loaf french bread
  • Real butter

Steps

  1. Cut andouille into bite-sized pieces
  2. Combine rix mix, season packet, andouille and 1tbsp butter in medium sauce pan over high heat
  3. Stir until well blended
  4. Bring to a rolling boil for 1 minute
  5. Reduce heat, cover and simmer until water is absorbed. Do not stir! Just wait.
  6. During last 5 minutes of cook, stir in shrimp
  7. When all water is absorbed, remove from heat
  8. Let rest for 5 minutes
  9. Fluff and serve with french bread and butter
  10. ENJOY!
