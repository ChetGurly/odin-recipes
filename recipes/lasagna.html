How to make delicious lasagna at home

Kirkland Signature Italian Sausage and Beef Frozen Lasagna

Making Kirkland Signature Italian Sausage and Beef Frozen Lasgna at home is simple and delicious! Simply follow these easy steps and you will be enjoying your Kirkland Signature Italian Sausage and Beef Frozen Lasagna in no time!

Ingredients:

  • Vehicle with sufficient fuel for your journey
  • Enough money to purchase lasagna (approximately $17 USD)
  • An oven

Steps

  1. Drive to Costco
  2. Purchase Kirkland Signature Italian Sausage and Beef frozen Lasagna
  3. Return home
  4. Preheat oven as directed on package
  5. Cook as directed on package
  6. ENJOY!
